❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you've answered fewer than 50 questions. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

Will I lose my data if my subscription expires?

No! Your learning records, review notebook, and achievements are saved permanently. You can access them again when you renew your subscription.

Can I use it on multiple devices?

Yes! You can log in on up to 2 devices simultaneously - mobile, tablet, or desktop.

How many questions can free users do?

Free users can answer up to 20 questions per day, limited to Vocabulary and Grammar only. Upgrade to Pro to unlock all topics and unlimited practice.

What extra features does Pro include?

Pro unlocks all topics (Listening/Speaking/Reading/Writing), unlimited practice, achievement system, study streak tracking, and ad-free experience.

How do I cancel auto-renewal?

You can cancel anytime in your Profile > Subscription settings. Your Pro benefits remain active until the end of your billing period.
